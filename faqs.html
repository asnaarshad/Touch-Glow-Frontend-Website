FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS


Who are we and what do we do?

Touch&Glow.pk is a family owned business that specializes in importing & retailing Original makeup through our website www.Touch&Glow.pk. We work with global makeup & beauty products manufacturers / distributors, internationally and provide the quality cosmetics / beauty products at the best prices available online across Pakistan. We have been in business since 2017 and have forged strong relationships with some of the largest global cosmetics distributors & manufacturers, which allow us to offer you the lowest priced items in Pakistan. Our Ecommerce platform enables us to serve all cities of Pakistan.

Do you Sell Original Products?

Yes we just deal in 100% original products only.

How Can I Verify the Originality of Products being sold at Touch&Glow.pk?

Most of the manufacturers have already mentioned us as their authentic retail Partners in Pakistan on their website & social media pages. You can always verify this from their Pages. Some of the High End Brands we buy from Sephora & other Trusted International Retailers in those cases our customers can always ask for the documents in case of confusions.

What is the Country of Manufacture of these Products?

Every brand has different country of manufacturing; some brands have multiple manufacturing countries E.g. same brand may have 1 items made from 1 country and other items from different countries. In some case the same item may have multiple countries making. The best way to verify this is confirm from the particular brand, or Wikipedia official profiles where they have mentioned this information.

What is your Average Delivery Time for shipping your Orders?

In most of the cases our average delivery time before Covid-19 Pandemics is 2-5 Working days after receiving your order. But in recent situation we promise 7-10 working days in order to not get under promise.

Can I Return or Exchange My Purchased Items for Any Reason?

Yes you can always return within 15 days of your purchase if you have not used or opened the product. You will have to send it back to us at your on cost of shipping; it will take 7-10 days to processes your refund / exchange after receiving back our products.
In case where the product is broken / used or opened we will decline the refund or exchange.

Are There Any Restock Charges?

Yes on standard exchanges and refunds we will charge you 20% of product cost as restock cost.

What happens in case if I have received any broken item in my Shipment?

We take best possible measures to pack your order in a very secure way, but since some make up items are very delicate so it can happen and in such cases you need to share the proof of damage within 3 hours of receiving those items and we will require 4-5 working days & we will compensate you in form of discount coupon as per the actual damage.

What types of payments are accepted?

1-Cash on Delivery (COD)
2-Bank Transfers (Advance)
3- Easy Paisa, Jazz Cash Etc.
4- Credit Cards (In Progress)

How can I place an order?

We only accept orders coming from our website (www.Touch&Glow.pk)
